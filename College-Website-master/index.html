Events

Technical Writing competition

The IEEE SJCIT Student Chapter's Technical Writing Competition is a platform for students to showcase their skills in presenting technical concepts effectively. It encourages innovation, critical thinking, and professional writing, fostering academic and industry readiness.

Avinya 2024 Hackathon

The IEEE SJCIT Student Chapter's Avinya 2024 Hackathon is a dynamic event where innovation meets collaboration. Participants tackle real-world challenges, develop creative solutions, and showcase technical expertise, fostering innovation and teamwork.
